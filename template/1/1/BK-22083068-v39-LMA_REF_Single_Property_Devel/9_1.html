Cost Overruns on some transactions are determined on an aggregate basis in which case an overrun on one  
line item in the Budgeted Costs could potentially be offset by a saving on another line item. In such cases  
there is no need to include the Cost Saving option. However on other transactions Cost Overruns are  
determined on a line by line basis. In such cases it is common to include a Cost Saving concept. The key  
difference between the two approaches is that a Cost Saving will only affect a Cost Overrun if approved by  
the relevant Lenders.
