(c)

7.8  
Restrictions on right to prepay or cancel  
Notwithstanding any other term of this Clause 7, the Borrower may not exercise any  
right of prepayment or cancellation under Clause 7.5 (Voluntary cancellation),  
Clause 7.6 (Voluntary prepayment of Loans) or Clause 7.7 (Right of repayment and  
cancellation in relation to a single Lender) until it has received confirmation from the  
Agent that the Majority Lenders are satisfied that, after prepayment or cancellation, the  
Borrower will have adequate funding to achieve Practical Completion.
